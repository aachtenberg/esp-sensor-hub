## Where to Buy

### ESP32 Boards
- Amazon (fast shipping)
- AliExpress (bulk pricing)
- Adafruit / SparkFun (quality, support)

### VE.Direct Cables
- Official Victron dealers
- [Victron Energy Store](https://www.victronenergy.com/accessories/ve-direct-cable)
- DIY: Buy 3.5mm TRRS connectors and make your own

### Power Supplies
- Amazon: Search "12V to 5V 3A waterproof micro USB car charger"
- eBay: Automotive section

### Enclosures
- Amazon: Search "IP65 junction box 100mm"
- Hardware stores: Electrical section
